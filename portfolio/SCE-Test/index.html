Colobuilders

When you choose Colobuilders, you decide to work with a company that has over thirty years of experience in building and managing data centers. When you work with us, you also take advantage of our superior knowledge of the industry and our excellent relationships with equipment and supply vendors around the world.

Colobuilders have been building data centers since 1986 and are the global leader in the industry, with 250 data centers in 24 countries on five continents and more than 7,200 employees. We offer highly customizable plans depending on your goals and budget. And we work with you closely to make sure that your project lines up with your vision. We also work hard to ensure that every step of the process is transparent.

Our strategically designed data center plans are customized to your specific needs at an affordable price while our data center management processes help you run your existing equipment more effectively. Through these, we improve efficiency and maximize your profits.
